Who am I?


My name is Juan Esteban Méndez Roys. I was born on November 8, 1997. I have lived my whole life with both of my parents, as well as my older brother. I am currently 21 years old. When I was less than 5 years old, I attended Mi Pequeño Mundo, my one and only kindergarden. I have some great memories from my time there, perhaps because it is the place where I made my first friends.

When I turned 5 years old, I started my school life at the Colegio San Carlos. A highly recognized school in Colombia, that taught me important life values that I will never forget. Values such as honesty, humbleness, and leadership; which I try to stay loyal to them on my daily basis. I learned many important things in this school. It prepared me very well for the different challenges that I will encounter in my future. At Colegio San Carlos I made some very loyal friends. I still keep in touch with them and I know they will always be there for me.

Once I graduated from school, I traveled away and lived by myself in Sydney, Australia for six months. This was an amazing experience I will never forget. I learned many things about me and about life in general. It made me grow as a human being. I met people and made friends from all over the world. I came close to many different cultures that I thought I was never going to have the opportunity to encounter. This experience truly made me an open-minded person, and reasured me to follow my dreams no matter at what cost.

I am currently sudying Systems and Computing Engineering at Universidad de los Andes. I am very happy with what I have learned here. I am currently finishing my third year, but I cannot wait for what life has waiting for me! Once I get my degree, I want to work in a cool tech-company for a while to gain some work experience. After that, my goal is to build my own start-up that can make life easier to all kinds of people around the world.

I knew the best engineering university in Colombia is the Universidad de los Andes, but I still was not sure this was where I wanted to study. When I graduated from school in 2015, I decided I did not want to start working on my undergraduate degree right away. This is why I decided to travel to Sydney, Australia for six months. In Sydney, I took intensive english classes at the English Language Company. I also took special lectures for preparing myself for the CAE (Cambridge Advanced English) exam, so that I could certify my english level internationally. I presented the CAE exam on December, 2015. I achieved a B1 english level certificate which made me very proud.

I came back to Bogotá, Colombia one month later. During my time in Australia I thought really hard on what I wanted to do with my life. I finally decided that I wanted to study Systems and Computing Engineering at the Universidad de los Andes in Bogota, Colombia. I am currently studying there and I am very happy and satisfied with what I have learned so far. I am sure I made the right decision and I am completely in love with the career I chose. It is a field with a lot of future and with many different branches I can work in. Personally, my favorite field is Software Engineering. Once I graduate I want to do a masters degree abroad on this field of computer science.

Chicago Cabs

Chicago Cabs was a project I made for my data structures course. I used some cool data structures such as hashtables, graphs, red black trees and heaps. For my final assignment I had to print a graph on Google Maps that connected all of the taxi trips made in a data range. All of the data was extracted from the Chicago government webpage.

AtPOS

AtPOS was my main project for my Software Architecture and Design course. While working on it, I learned how to implement a documental database on MongoDB. I used Amazon Web Services for my first time. I implemented some very famous architectural styles and patterns. It was the first project that consisted on a distributed application I have ever made. I configured a load balancer for my first time, as well as an API gateway on Kong. I learned a bit about containers on Docker. It was my first project developed on Python and Ruby. My team used the Django and Ruby on Rails frameworks.

Visit Repository
Safe Channel

The Safe Channel project was about establishing a secure connection between two parties. I implemented encryption for my first time. I used different cyber security technics such as symmetric and assymetric encryption, digital signatures, and message authentication codes (MAC).

Visit Repository
Used Cars

Used Cars project consisted on developing a web application for selling used cars. I worked with an experienced development team. I learned amazing new concepts such as API RestFUL services and how to develope a three-layer architecture application. This project made me start using modern technologies such as Git, GitHub, Jenkins, SonarQube, and PostMan.
