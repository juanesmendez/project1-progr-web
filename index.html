Who am I?

My name is Juan Esteban Méndez Roys. I was born on November 8, 1997. My parents are Emilio Méndez and Nilva Roys. I have lived my whole life with both of my parents, as well as my older brother. I am currently 21 years old. When I was less than 5 years old, I attended Mi Pequeño Mundo, my one and only kindergarden. I have some great memories from my time there, perhaps because it is the place where I made my first friends. When I turned 5 years old, I started my school life at the Colegio San Carlos. A highly recognized school in Colombia.
